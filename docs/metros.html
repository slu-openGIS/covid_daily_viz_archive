Metropolitan Areas

The following plots and maps focus on all of Missouri’s metropolitan statistical areas (MSA). These areas include portions of Illinois (in the case of the Cape Girardeau and St. Louis metros), Kansas (in the case of the Kansas City and St. Joseph metros), and Oklahoma (the Joplin metro). The FAQ contains more details about what constitutes a MSA.
